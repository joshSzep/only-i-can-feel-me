# The Recruit

The recruit is the central point-of-view character.

They encounter HumansRHumans during a period of personal vulnerability.

Possible motivations:

- loneliness
- grief
- disillusionment
- moral confusion
- search for belonging

The movement initially feels like refuge.

It provides:

- community
- clarity
- purpose
- identity

---

## Radicalization Arc

1. curiosity
2. participation
3. loyalty
4. ideological commitment
5. moral compromise
6. doubt
7. realization

The recruit’s final realization is that the movement has become detached from its original purpose.
