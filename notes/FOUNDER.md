# Founder

The founder of HumansRHumans originates from the Sangha community where concerns about empathy technology first emerged.

They are contemplative, thoughtful, and philosophically serious.

Their original belief:

Human consciousness should not be technologically mediated.

They do not initially advocate violence or extremism.

Their goal is to protect the integrity of inner life.

---

## Philosophy

The founder believes:

- empathy should be earned through human relationship
- emotional experience should remain sovereign
- machines should not access human interiority

Their position is grounded in spiritual and philosophical concerns.

---

## Tragedy

After a traumatic event involving empathy technology, the founder helps create HumansRHumans.

The movement begins as a resistance group.

---

## Loss of Control

As HumansRHumans spreads online, the founder gradually loses influence.

New factions emerge.

The ideology becomes more extreme.

The founder eventually realizes the movement has become something they did not intend.
