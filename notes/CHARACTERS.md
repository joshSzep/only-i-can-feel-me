# Characters

## The Recruit

The protagonist of the novel.

A person searching for meaning, belonging, or moral clarity.

Gradually becomes radicalized through involvement with HumansRHumans.

Eventually realizes the movement has become something different from its origins.

---

## The Founder

The originator of HumansRHumans.

Originally part of the Sangha community.

Philosophically serious and morally thoughtful.

Opposes empathy technology but does not support violent extremism.

Eventually realizes the movement has escaped their control.

---

## Early Sangha Members

Individuals who participated in the philosophical debates before the movement formed.

Some remain contemplative critics of the technology.

Others join the radicalizing movement.

---

## Internet-Era Activists

Members who join HumansRHumans through online spaces.

They lack the original philosophical grounding.

Often more ideological and extreme.

---

## Splinter Faction Leaders

Individuals who lead decentralized cells.

Interpret the ideology in different ways.

Some seek peaceful resistance.

Others advocate aggressive action.
