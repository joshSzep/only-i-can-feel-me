# HumansRHumans

HumansRHumans is a decentralized resistance movement opposing empathy technology.

Its core belief:

Human consciousness should never be machine-mediated.

---

## Origin

The movement emerges from a contemplative community after a tragic event involving empathy technology.

Initially the movement seeks:

- public awareness
- ethical debate
- technological restraint

---

## Structure

The movement begins small and organized.

Over time it becomes decentralized through online communication.

Cells operate independently.

Leadership becomes diffuse.

---

## Ideological Drift

As the movement spreads online:

- slogans replace philosophy
- ideas become simplified
- factions diverge

Some cells adopt extreme interpretations.

The movement gradually loses ideological coherence.
