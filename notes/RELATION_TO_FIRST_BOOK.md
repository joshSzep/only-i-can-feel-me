# Relation to What It Feels Like to Be You

*Only I Can Feel Me* takes place in the same universe as *What It Feels Like to Be You*.

The books explore two sides of the same philosophical question.

---

## What It Feels Like to Be You

Explores:

- the creation of empathy technology
- its impact on society
- the debate around its ethics
- the eventual regulation of the technology

---

## Only I Can Feel Me

Explores:

- the resistance movement opposing the technology
- the ideological evolution of HumansRHumans
- the psychology of radicalization
- how movements change once they grow beyond their founders

---

The two books form a philosophical pair.

One explores shared experience.

The other explores the defense of individual interiority.
